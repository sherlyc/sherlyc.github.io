Time & Habits

Which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

Timebox, pormodoro technique and tiny habit.

what did you find interesting?

These are really useful to keep myself making progress with my coursework. By breaking the task into 25 minutes chunks and take a small break after every 25 minutes, wish I have known this technique earlier.

What worked for you?

Timeboxing establish a time limit for a work in progress, forces me to prioritize and perform the small amount of work that matters most, this sharpens my mind in getting something valuable done quickly.

Which will you experiment further with?

I will look into the meditation technique, perhaps using the headspace app and see what it can do.
