Daily Mindfulness

What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

Just paying my full attention to someone by listening made that someone feels important and being appreciated. That person feels happier and our relationship is improved.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

Walking meditation seems like a good choice for me as it's quite easy for me to fall asleep when I am doing the sitting meditation. Walking meditation sounded easy and achievable and I can take the opportunity to go out, get some fresh air plus exercising.
