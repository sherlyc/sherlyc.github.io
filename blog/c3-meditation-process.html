Some thoughts after reading Chapter 2 of Search Inside Yourself

Never have I thought that meditation is so easy and it seems like if I could mastered meditation, it would help me in every aspect of life. I am thrilled!

I tend to get stressed easily and I have the tendency to run away from problem instead of facing it. With the help of meditation, I can learn to manage my stress, overcome my fear and achieve happiness.

One of the techniques mentioned in a book is to imagine seeing a butterfly resting on a flower with a gentle breeze (our breaths) lifting and lower it. I love it and found it a great aid in improving my concentration

I also love the idea of seeing myself in the loving eyes of grandmother, I will try to practise it. Thank you for your attention.
